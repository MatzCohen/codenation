A little about me...



My name is Matz Cohen, I live in London and currently looking to revive my passion in computer programming.
I am a collage graduate with a Diploma in Mechanical & Electrical engineering, I am very technically minded and always found it easy to “talk technology”.

Nothing more than a hobby

About 30 years ago I got my first PC and remember till today how I stayed up all night playing with it. I was so fascinated by it that I very quickly taught myself how to develop simple programs using the BASIC programming language.
Bit by bit I was attempting bigger projects and very soon after, I was able to help a family friend who owned a retail shop selling shoes, with a program that helped him manage all aspects of his business, just by using a database application called dBase. The family friend used the software for many years and always commented how good it was.
At the time, I was working in a partnership with my 3 brothers, a successful business but nothing to do with computers, and kept programming only as a hobby. Soon after that, I got married, had kids and my hobby was less important in my life.

A passion revived

When last month, I was introduced to Code Nation, I found the opportunity to take a course in Python programming.
I was excited as it brought back many wonderful memories even though I did not have a clue what this language was.
From the first day my eyes were glued to the monitor and took to it like duck to water, It was like I knew some Latin and now learing Italian!
I am still amazed that after only one week, I was able to write close to 500 lines of working code, in Python – a language I never knew existed a week before.
By the second week I was creating a website by actually writing HTML and CSS code for the first time as opposed to using various website design platforms that hide the code behind a graphic interface.
I found this experience exhilarating and gave me the need to learn more and try out different ways of writing a procedure code.

Not just a hobby

I now hope to re-connect with my long-time hobby and turn it into a source of income as well as a satisfying job.

I will look to advance my knowledge in computer programming and website design through taking more advanced courses and self-learn from the vast amount of information available today online.
