Unit 1 | Outcome 3

Improving Productivity with IT

Role : Technical Consultant


Develop and test solutions to improve ongoing use of IT tools and systems



Scenario

Continued from previous task.

3.1 Review the benefits and drawbacks of IT tools and systems used in terms of productivity and efficiency – could you have been more efficient when making your website? How? Could you have done it in teams?


The task of making a website single-handed or in a team has different requirements of IT tools and systems.
When we work as a team, we need more communication and project management tools. These are not that necessary when the work is done by one person, except perhaps a scheduling tool to keep track of work to be done.
If I was working as a team building my website, I would have delegated the work according to team ability and would have expected a more efficient effort.

3.2 Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency


You can improve productivity and efficiency by doing the following:

  • Plan your week in advance
  • Delegate responsibility
  • Think positively
  • Communicate effectively
  • Hold stand-up meetings
  • Be a good example
  • Give them incentives
  • Use project management tools
  • Integrate technology

3.3 Develop solutions to improve own productivity using IT in digital projects – what would you do differently next time? Team work? Use of Slack?


To improve my own productivity, I would plan as much as I can.
When working within a team, I would make use of communication tools like Slack and Zoom to get everyone involved and show the benefit and rewards of a team effort

3.4 Describe how you would go about testing digital solutions


The more popular it is the more information there is about it online
I would go to various online forums and review websites and sites like YouTube, Bing, and Google to get as much information as possible. If required I would consider taking special training on a particular technology to be able to use it more efficiently
